NODE

NODE is a network that makes art work.

We collaborate, conspire, and push boundaries to help artists create unforgettable experiences.

We’re dreamers and misfits who don’t care much about the rules (even the ones we invent). We reject the midcurve – the safe, the obvious, the boring.

We believe that art is not separate from technology – it’s the force that makes it matter. We embrace this contradiction and welcome anyone who doesn’t quite fit in to join us.

The future is a network. The future is ours.
